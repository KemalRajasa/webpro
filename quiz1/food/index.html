Surabaya, The Heroes City

Written by: Kemal Tangguh Aji Rajasa | 24 September 2025

Surabaya is not only known for its historical significance but also for its vibrant culinary scene.

The city boasts a wide array of traditional dishes that reflect its rich cultural heritage, but one main component that stands out in Surabaya's cuisine is its bold and spicy flavors, often incorporating generous amounts of chili and local spices.

The city is known for its culinary delights, including local dishes such as "rawon", "rujak cingur", and "pecel". Surabaya also has a vibrant street food scene, that is commonly found in areas like Tunjungan Street and G-Walk.
