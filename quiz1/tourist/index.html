Surabaya, The Heroes City

Written by: Kemal Tangguh Aji Rajasa | 24 September 2025

There are many reasons to why Surabaya is a great city, you could mention its historical significance, culinary, universities, economy, culture et cetera, but one thing surabaya is not known for is its tourist attractions.

There were alot of tourist attraction in java island, but not many in surabaya itself. However, there are still some places you could visit when you are in surabaya, such as the following: Mangrove Ecotourism, Surabaya Zoo, House of Sampoerna, and Submarine Monument.

One could say Surabaya is a busy city, with everything going fast paced, locals usually spend their weekend at malls shopping, so tourist attractions were not really a thing here
